Broken For a Purpose

by Ayanna Walker

Everyone encounters obstacles in day to day life, things that can break a person and cripple them mentally, physically and emotionally. This book was written to reach every broken soul struggling to find a purpose to their pain. This is for everyone who needs some motivation, some reassurance that although storms may come and life may fling you from pillar to post, you are fully equipped to weather the storm and become a better person because of it. The author shares her experiences hoping that those experiences will speak to someone who needs to know that every struggle in life happens for a reason. The goal is to use words to resonate in some way in the life of someone who needs to know they are not alone, to know that they are not fighting a losing battle.
